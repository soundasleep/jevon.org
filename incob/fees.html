Payment of Fees

Payment of fees must be remitted with the registration form. Payments can be made by cheque or via credit card. For foreign participants, this must be done with an international bank cheque drawn on a bank in New Zealand. All cheques should be made payable to "Massey University".

Registration and acceptance of abstracts will only be confirmed once payment has been received.
