A two day pre-conference workshop will be held from the 2nd - 3rd September at the Albany Campus of Massey University. Participation is limited to 60 participants only on a first-come first-served basis.

The pre-conference Workshop will be held at the Albany campus of Massey University (about a 30 miniute drive from the middle of Auckland). The venue will be AT5 (seminar room) and AT4 (computer suite) in The Atrium. How to find The Atrium: enter Massey campus off highway 17 at entrance 1, go through the main gate and in front of you you will see a parking area. The Atrium is the round building directly ahead of you, slightly to the right.

A van will be leaving The Carlton (middle of town) at 8.20am on the morning of 2nd and 3rd September to transport those who are in town to the venue. Please meet in the lobby of The Carlton at 8.15am for an 8.20am departure.
