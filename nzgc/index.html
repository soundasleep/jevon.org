Introduction

The Allan Wilson Centre for Molecular Ecology and Evolution (AWC) which is hosted by Massey University has formed the New Zealand Genome Centre (NZGC) with associated equipment and staff, to undertake genome projects of significant value to New Zealand.

The NZGC is a repository of genomic information which has been gathered by scientists and their postgraduate students in New Zealand.
